Physics HL Paper 1

May 2024 TZ1 — Question 8

An electric motor has 1.8 kJ of energy transferred to it in 0.50 minutes. The efficiency of the motor is 40%. What is the useful power output?
A. 1.5 W   B. 24 W   C. 150 W   D. 360 W

Tags: Energy ¬∑ Power

Answer: B. 24 W

AI reasoning: 1.8 kJ / 0.50 min = 1800 J / 30 s = 60 W. With 40% efficiency: 0.40 × 60 W = 24 W. The only option matching 24 W is B.
